Most clients begin seeing positive responses within the first two weeks of campaign launch. Initial meetings typically start in weeks 3-4, with first deals entering your pipeline by month 2. We maintain enterprise-level deliverability through dedicated Azure servers with industry-specific IP warming protocols, advanced domain authentication (DKIM, SPF, DMARC), and proprietary sending pattern algorithms tailored to financial services communication standards. Our redundant infrastructure includes shadow warmup sequences, real-time blacklist monitoring, and automated failover systems specifically engineered for investment banking outreach patterns.
For every outreach email sent, we automatically send 10 warmup emails to maintain maximum deliverability. This means if you're campaigns involve 10,000 outreach emails monthly, you will be sending 110,000 total emails (100,000 warmup + 10,000 outreach) to ensure optimal inbox placement and domain reputation management. Please note that our pricing scales with every email sent, regardless on whether it is warmup or outreach.
Our platform is built to meet stringent financial industry standards including FINRA/SEC-safe messaging logic and full GDPR/CAN-SPAM alignment. We maintain compliant record-keeping systems and ensure all communication follows regulatory guidelines specific to investment banking and private equity outreach.
